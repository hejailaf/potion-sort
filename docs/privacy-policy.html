Potion Sort — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Potion Sort collects no data. Full stop.

  • No account is required and none can be created.
  • All game progress (levels, coins, settings) is stored only on your device and never leaves it.
  • The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and makes no network requests of its own.
  • We collect no personal information, device identifiers, or usage data.

If you install a beta version through Apple TestFlight, Apple may share crash logs and feedback you choose to submit with the developer, under Apple's own privacy terms.

Questions? Contact: hejailaf@gmail.com

Potion Sort is an independent game. This policy applies to all versions of the app.
