# Release Notes

## v1.3.0 — 2026-07-10

**New mechanics**
- Veiled Bottles (level 20) — a veil hides what's inside; cork any bottle to lift one.
- Mystery Potions (level 40) — only the top potion is known; pour it away to reveal what's below.
- Sealed Bottles (level 60) — a wax seal locks a bottle shut; every pour on the board weakens it until it pops open.

**Journey tab** — the full road from level 1 to your next milestone; unlocked mechanics get
a gold milestone disc you can tap any time to recall its explainer.

**Feel** — hand-measured pour choreography (tilt, stream, fill), a cork-drop + ribbon/particle
celebration on completion, a reworked coin-fly with per-coin haptics, Home↔Journey crossfade,
board deal-in, and consistent press states throughout.

**Difficulty** — the curve now reaches 12 bottles / 10 colors at level 38+.

**Internal** — 173 tests green (engine + store logic); parked ideas tracked in BACKLOG.md.
